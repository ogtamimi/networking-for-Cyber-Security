# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We aim to make this project a welcoming and educational space for everyone interested in networking and cybersecurity.

---

## Expected Behavior

All participants are expected to:

- Be respectful and professional
- Use clear and constructive language
- Respect different experience levels (beginners are welcome)
- Focus on learning and improvement

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not allowed:

- Harassment or insults
- Toxic or disrespectful comments
- Spam or irrelevant contributions
- Disruptive or offensive content

---

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behavior may result in:

- Comment removal
- Pull request rejection
- Temporary or permanent ban from contributing

---

## Goal

This project exists to help people understand networking for cybersecurity in a simple and practical way. Let’s keep it focused, clean, and useful for everyone.
